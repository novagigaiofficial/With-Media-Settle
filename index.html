ABOUT WITHMEDIA

Where cinema meets social media. Every frame, every cut, every second of sound — built to perform.

WithMedia is a cinematic content production studio founded by Manish Kumar P S in Hyderabad in 2020. We work with creators, coaches, music artists, and brands to build social-first content that performs — not just looks good. Over the last seven years we have grown 3M+ followers across client accounts, produced 1,000+ videos, and generated more than 50 million organic views.

01
CINEMA FIRST

Story decides the cut, not the platform. Every edit starts with the question a filmmaker would ask: what’s the story? Platform comes second. Story always comes first.

02
DATA DRIVEN

Retention curves and hook rates drive every iteration. Beautiful content that doesn’t perform is a waste. We study the numbers to make every video smarter.
