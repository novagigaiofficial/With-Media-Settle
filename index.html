Our Story
ABOUT WITHMEDIA
We believe social media deserves cinematic attention. Every frame, every cut, every second of sound — it all matters.

WithMedia was founded in 2017 in Hyderabad with one stubborn conviction: the gap between cinema and social media content shouldn't exist. The best creators deserve the same production quality and creative intent that goes into films — compressed into 30 seconds and built to stop a scroll.

01
CINEMA FIRST

Every edit starts with the question a filmmaker would ask: what's the story? Platform comes second. Story comes first.

02
DATA DRIVEN

Beautiful content that doesn't perform is a waste. We study retention curves, hook rates, and engagement to make every video smarter.

03
NO TEMPLATES

Every creator has a different voice, niche, and audience. We build content systems from scratch, not from a library of re-used formats.

The Person Behind the Work
FOUNDER
Manish Kumar P S
Founder & Creative Director

I started WithMedia in 2017 with a camera, DaVinci Resolve, and a belief that social media content could be treated with the same seriousness as cinema. Seven years later, we've grown 3M+ followers, produced 1,000+ videos, and generated over 50 million views — all from Hyderabad.

My background is in cinematic storytelling — I shoot on a Sony FX30, grade in S-Log3, and think about content the way a cinematographer thinks about a shot: intention first, execution second. I'm influenced by Tarantino's editing rhythm, Kubrick's visual precision, and the emotional storytelling of One Piece.

At WithMedia, I lead all creative direction and strategy. Every client brief crosses my desk. Every edit goes through my eyes. The team handles production at scale, but the creative vision stays consistent — because it runs through a single point of responsibility.

When I'm not in the edit, I'm building systems — workflow tools, content pipelines, automation. The best creative agencies run like machines behind the scenes so they can be purely creative in front of the camera.
